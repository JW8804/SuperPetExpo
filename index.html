What is Super Pet Expo?

Super Pet Expo brings together pet owners, animal lovers, vendors, and rescue organizations for an all-in-one event experience. Explore new products, enjoy live activities, and connect with people who care about animals as much as you do.

What You'll Find

From shopping to adoption opportunities, this event is built for discovery and fun.

  • Shopping & Gear: Treats, toys, accessories, nutrition, grooming, and more—browse vendors and find new favorites.
  • Rescues & Adoption: Meet rescue organizations and learn how to support adoption and animal welfare.
  • Live Experiences: Demos, activities, and moments designed to be fun, interactive, and photo-worthy.
